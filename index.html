About Me

Hi, I'm Sajid Ali from India and Here is my short summary of love towards my passion, I started my art Journey in my schooling. During my college days, I was more connected physically and mentally with my passion.Then, I started to explore and work hard on the artwork. I was specialize in hand drawing and focused on uniqueness with creativity... Passion on art leads me to focus on different perspective in each and every content. My artwork shows a unique, finest, simple and creative ... I'm so grateful to my passion and I'm committed to continuing my artistic journey, pushing the boundaries of my creativity and sharing my unique world creation with my people who is near and far.
"Art enables us to find ourselves and lose ourselves at the same time - Thomas Merton"
His words hit me everytime that's true, which reflects my love towards art
